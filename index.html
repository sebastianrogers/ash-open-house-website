About Us

Ash Open House is a small Christian cell group that meets regularly in Ash, Kent. We are a community of believers who gather together to:

  • Study the Bible and grow in faith
  • Pray and support one another
  • Share fellowship and friendship
  • Serve our local community

Everyone is welcome, whether you're new to faith or have been a Christian for many years. We value authenticity, grace, and love.
